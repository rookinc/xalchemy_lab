# G900 Phi Extension Probe v0.1

## Goal
Test whether phi or a simple phi-derived dimensionless form directly matches the current G900 extension ratios.

## Ratios tested
- d / I
- L / I
- d / L
- and their inverses where useful

## Interpretation rule
A clean direct match would support phi as a direct extension normalizer.
A miss does not eliminate phi entirely; it may still govern upstream 5-side geometry rather than the final normalized outputs.

## Present stance
Phi remains a live extension-scalar hypothesis, but must earn its role through geometric or ratio-level evidence.
