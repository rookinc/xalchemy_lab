# G60 North Face East Sector Perturbation

## Carrier

- `north_face_center`

## First lawful arrival

- `north_east_sector_perturbation`

## Meaning

The first framed G60 patch is anchored at the north face center.

The first asymmetry is introduced by perturbing the eastern sector of the north face.

This provides the first non-neutral local arrival into the North patch and gives the chart a reason to distinguish continuation classes.

## Working interpretation

North is the local carrier.
East-on-North is the first disturbance.
The framed readout is taken after this perturbation.

## Strongest sentence

The first lawful arrival into the North patch is a perturbation sourced from the eastern sector of the north face.
