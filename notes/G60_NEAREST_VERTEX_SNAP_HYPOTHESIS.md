# G60 Nearest-Vertex Snap Hypothesis

## Hypothesis

When multiple face perturbations induce a reduced interior perturbation, the resulting interior state is resolved by snapping to the nearest G60 vertex.

## Structural picture

1. boundary faces are perturbed
2. their effects combine into an interior bias
3. the interior bias is discretized by nearest-vertex snap in G60

## Interpretation

The reduced interior state need not remain a continuous or ambiguous interior quantity.

G60 supplies a discrete admissible readout by selecting the nearest vertex.

## Strongest sentence

The interior reduced perturbation is read by snapping to the nearest G60 vertex.

## Consequence

This gives a natural bridge between:

- continuous-looking interior reduction
- discrete G60 transport structure
