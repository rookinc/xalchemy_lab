# G60 Non-Retracing Closure Refinement

## Refinement

The first honest non-retracing closure is not rigidly:

- center -> face -> corner -> center

in every case.

A better rule is:

- center
- perturbation site
- nearest lawful non-retracing continuation site
- center

## Interpretation

The turning site depends on the location of the perturbation relative to the anchored support geometry.

If the perturbation is biased toward a corner sector, the return may turn through a corner.

If the perturbation remains closer to a face-centered continuation class, the return may stay face-centered longer before closing.

## Strongest sentence

The return prefers the nearest lawful non-retracing continuation site relative to the perturbation.

## Consequence

The G60 closure picture should be treated as a family of local closure motifs rather than one rigid universal path.
