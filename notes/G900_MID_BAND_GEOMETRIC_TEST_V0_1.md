# G900 Mid-Band Geometric Test v0.1

## Goal
Test the leading geometric explanation for the extracted offset magnitude d = 5.

## Hypothesis
The weighted prism normalization comes from a face-to-face band of thickness equal to the carrier layer count:

- total band thickness = 10
- midpoint rung = 145
- half-thickness = 5
- face weights = 145 - 5 and 145 + 5

So the extracted law

- (140, 145, 150)

would be the centered half-gap realization of a 10-layer band.

## What would support this
Evidence should show that:

1. the two face strata are opposite ends of a 10-step or 10-layer separation,
2. the rung is the midpoint support between them,
3. the exported normalization records half-thickness around the midpoint.

## What would weaken this
The hypothesis weakens if the same offset 5 can appear without any meaningful 10-layer midpoint geometry.
