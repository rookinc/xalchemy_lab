## Downstairs staged grammar
From current G900 extraction:

## Provisional map
- A -> launch
- B -> commit
- C -> reconcile

## Why B maps to commit
B is not merely arbitrary branching.
It changes transport mode, branch, or twist class.

That is structurally closer to:
- committing to a transport regime
- selecting a lane
- entering a stabilized transport mode

than to either first launch or final closure.

## Why C maps to reconcile
C is explicitly defined as:
- close
- coherence-restoring closure
- return-identification

That is the closest upstairs role yet found to the downstairs reconcile role.
