## Candidate theorem
Let the current G900 descent satisfy:

1. the carrier has 10 inward layers;
2. the midpoint regime occurs at layer 5;
3. layer 4 is the unique predecessor shell immediately before midpoint;
4. the centered prism mediator has already been forced to carry the center 145;
5. the off-center prism branches inherit their displacement from predecessor-shell position.

Then the branch displacement is forced to be:

- d = 10 / 2 = 5

Therefore the exact centered prism law is:

- (145 - 5, 145, 145 + 5) = (140,145,150)
