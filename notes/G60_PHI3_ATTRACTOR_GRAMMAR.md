# G60 Phi^3 Attractor Grammar

## Principle

Use `phi^3` only to set the initial attractor vertex.

After attractor selection, the remainder of the transport is governed by known grammar.

## Cycle

1. face perturbation occurs
2. a tri-turtle packet is formed
3. the packet is valued at `phi^3`
4. the field resolves to the nearest G60 attractor vertex
5. from that attractor, the known fastest path back to center is followed
6. the closure receipt indexes a finite library of next face states

## Strongest sentence

Use `phi^3` to set the attractor vertex; after that, transport follows known grammar.

## Consequence

The hard problem is reduced to attractor selection.
Once the attractor is known, closure and re-emission can be handled by a finite library.
