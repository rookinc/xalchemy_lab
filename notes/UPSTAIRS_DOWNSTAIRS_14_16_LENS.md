# Upstairs / Downstairs 14:16 Lens

## Hypothesis

The ratio `14:16` is better interpreted as an upstairs/downstairs structural split than as a direct resonance scalar.

## Working interpretation

- downstairs = reduced / center-facing / return grammar layer
- upstairs = lifted / face-facing / radiative layer

The ratio `14:16 = 7:8` may indicate a first occupancy, admissibility, or active-state split between these two layers.

## Phi relation

A natural companion lens is:

- `phi - 1 = 1/phi` for contraction / reduction / downstairs
- `phi + 1 = phi^2` for expansion / lift / upstairs

So the cycle may involve a phi-mediated exchange between an upstairs and downstairs register.

## Strongest sentence

`14:16` feels like an upstairs/downstairs split, while `phi ± 1` feels like the expansion/contraction law relating the two.

## Status

This is a structural lens to test, not yet a proved derivation.
