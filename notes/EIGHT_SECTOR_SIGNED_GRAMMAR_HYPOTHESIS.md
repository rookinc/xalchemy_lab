# Eight-Sector Signed Grammar Hypothesis

## Hypothesis

The cube has 8 internal sectors.

Wherever the spark appears, the internal sector orientation determines the signed lift+twist grammar needed for reconciliation in one move.

## Interpretation

The kernel need not derive reconciliation from scratch each time.

Instead:

1. locate the spark's internal sector
2. read the sector orientation
3. apply the signed lift+twist grammar prescribed by that sector

## Strongest sentence

The spark's internal sector determines the signed lift+twist grammar in one move.

## Consequence

The reconciliation half-step can be implemented as a finite lookup over 8 sector classes.
