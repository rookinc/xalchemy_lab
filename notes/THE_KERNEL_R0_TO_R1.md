# The Kernel: r0 -> r1

## Statement

The kernel of the system is the transition:

- `r0 -> r1`

## Interpretation

This is the unique nontrivial step in which:

- a raw perturbation resolves to a location
- the cube orients its centroid to that location
- the initial propagation state is fixed

After `r1`, the remaining evolution is mostly deterministic grammar.

## Strongest sentence

The kernel is the map from raw perturbation to resolved centroid-oriented initial state.

## Short form

The kernel is:

- `r0 -> r1`
