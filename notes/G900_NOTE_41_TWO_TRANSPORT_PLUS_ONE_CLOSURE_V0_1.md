# G900 Note 41 — Two Transport Roles Plus One Closure Role v0.1

## Proposal
The current G900 mechanism is not best modeled as three peer-level transport families.

It is better modeled as:

- two transport roles
- one closure role

## Candidate split
### Transport layer
- F1 = live directed shift
- F2 = same-macro continuation / hold

### Closure layer
- B = midpoint / pre-midpoint renormalized closure

## Why this split is needed
The carrier evidence supports:
- one live directed shift family
- one dominant same-macro family
- one absent opposite shift family

But the centered signed prism law only appears after renormalization.

So the third role is not behaving like a third raw edge family.
It is behaving like a closure map.

## Meaning
The grammar is stratified, not flat.

It is better described as:

- transport
- transport
- closure

rather than as three equivalent transport moves.

## Honest status
This is a structural role hypothesis, not yet a theorem.
