# Spark–Photon Conjecture

## Conjecture

A spark is the minimal perturbation event of the model.

There exists a regime in which a spark may be interpreted as a photon-like boundary excitation.

## Conservative reading

This does not claim that every spark is a photon.
It does not claim that the model has already derived photon physics.

It claims only that the formal spark object may correspond to a photon-like excitation in an appropriate physical regime.

## Strongest sentence

A spark is the formal perturbation primitive of the model, and may admit a photon-like physical interpretation.

## Role in the model

The spark is the input to the kernel:

- `r0 -> r1`

So if the conjecture is right, a photon-like excitation would be the minimal physical event that seeds the kernel transition.
