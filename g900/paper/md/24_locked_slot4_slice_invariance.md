# Locked Slot-4 Slice Invariance

## Status
Working theorem note

## Purpose
Formalize the distinction between the full normalized frame-2 seam and the distinguished slot-4 slice generated by the locked witnesses.

---

## 1. Exact prototype

Let
\[
E_2=[o4,s0,t0,s2,t2,s4]
\]
be the normalized exact frame-2 prototype.

---

## 2. Slot-4 slice

Define the slot-4 slice by
\[
\Sigma_2^{(4)}
:=
\{c\in\Sigma_2^{\mathrm{full}}:\ c_k=(E_2)_k \text{ for all }k\neq 4\}.
\]

By the exact seam family theorem,
\[
\Sigma_2^{(4)}
=
\{[o4,s0,t0,s2,x,s4]:
x\in\{o4,s0,s2,s3,s4,t0,t3,t4\}\}.
\]

This is the distinguished one-coordinate seam family selected in the bounded obstruction analysis.

---

## 3. Locked witness set

The locked witness set is
\[
L=
\{
[o4,s0,t0,s2,t4,s4],\,
[o4,s0,t0,s2,o4,s4],\,
[o4,s0,t0,s2,s3,s4],\,
[o4,s0,t0,s2,s0,s4]
\}.
\]

Thus
\[
L\subseteq \Sigma_2^{(4)}.
\]

So the locked witnesses lie entirely in the slot-4 slice.

---

## 4. Locked seam slice generated by \(L\)

Let
\[
\Sigma_2^{L}
\]
denote the subset of the full normalized frame-2 seam that is reachable from \(L\) under the retained seam dynamics.

The computational evidence from the locked-automaton and seam-family verifications shows that the reachable seam family from \(L\) is precisely
\[
[o4,s0,t0,s2,x,s4]
\]
with
\[
x\in\{o4,s0,s2,s3,s4,t0,t3,t4\}.
\]

Thus the reachable seam family from \(L\) stays inside the slot-4 slice.

---

## 5. Invariance statement

### Proposition 5.1. Locked slot-4 slice invariance
The seam dynamics generated from the locked witnesses preserve the slot-4 slice:
\[
\Sigma_2^{L}\subseteq \Sigma_2^{(4)}.
\]

### Stronger expected form
The evidence suggests the stronger equality
\[
\Sigma_2^{L}= \Sigma_2^{(4)}.
\]

This says that the locked witness regime generates the full slot-4 slice and no other coordinate slice of the full frame-2 seam.

---

## 6. Interpretation

The bounded obstruction studied in this paper is not a theorem about the whole normalized frame-2 seam. It is a theorem about a distinguished invariant slice of that seam.

The role of the locked witnesses is therefore structural: they select the slot-4 slice, and the retained dynamics from those witnesses remain inside it.

So the exact seam family theorem is best understood as a theorem about a dynamically selected coordinate slice of the full seam.

---

## 7. Main open question

The central remaining structural question is:

> why do the locked witness dynamics preserve the slot-4 slice rather than mixing into the other coordinate slices of the full frame-2 seam?

This is now the cleanest formulation of the remaining explanation problem.
