## Immediate readings of the table

### Binary rung
What Binary contributes is not size or graph richness.
It contributes a clean example of:

- one lawful generator
- two natural normalization frames
- one exact relation between the resulting images
- one derived asymptotic consequence

This makes it the prototype rung.

### Thalean rung
What Thalean contributes is the first fully worked nontrivial finite object in which:

- local admissible transport exists
- quotient descent matters
- the image \(Q=MM^T\) is exact
- and the image closes polynomially in a quotient-core operator

This makes it the canonical operator rung.

### G900 rung
What G900 contributes is scale and ambition.
But right now it contributes mostly open columns.

That is fine.
The whole point of the table is to make those blanks explicit.

## Non-negotiable discipline

Do not fill G900 with wishful symmetry.

A G900 cell stays blank unless one of the following exists:

- explicit computation
- exact reduction
- stable repeated measurement
- or a clearly marked conjectural candidate

Use labels like:

- KNOWN
- CANDIDATE
- OPEN
- FAILED
- REJECTED

if the table starts to sprawl.
